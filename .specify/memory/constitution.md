# Egbe Jahari Website Constitution

## Core Principles

### I. Seriedade e Reverência
The design and content MUST reflect the serious nature of the spiritual tradition. Avoid playful, overly casual, or distracting elements to maintain the required solemnity.

### II. Amor pela Espiritualidade
The website MUST foster a sense of connection, peace, and spiritual depth through its aesthetics, utilizing harmonious color palettes and modern, elegant typography.

### III. Respeito à Tradição Viva
Content MUST honor the elders, history, and active practitioners. Accuracy and respect in presenting rituals, beliefs, and history are NON-NEGOTIABLE.

### IV. Acessibilidade e Clareza
Information MUST be accessible to both community members and those seeking to learn, maintaining clarity without diluting the tradition's depth.

## Design Aesthetics

Use curated, harmonious color palettes reflecting spirituality and tradition (e.g., deep earthy tones, serene blues, or pristine whites depending on the specific tradition's symbolism). Typography should be modern and read elegantly. Subtle micro-animations that feel organic can be used, but must not be flashy. Rich imagery that conveys respect and solemnity is required.

## Content Guidelines

All texts must be reviewed for doctrinal accuracy and respectful tone. Images of rituals or sacred objects must be approved by community leaders before publication.

## Governance

This Constitution supersedes all other design and development practices. Amendments to these principles require approval from the community leadership. All code, design, and content changes must be reviewed against these principles to ensure compliance.
